Home

This page is the Home page of GreenHouse. From this page users can access the My House, Care Calendar, Plant Search, Add Plant, and Settings pages. Each plant on the home page is also a clickable link giving information about the plant.

For example, clicking on a Sky Plant to will open a box with information on the Sky Plant. Clicking this box will bring the user to the Plant Search section of the app where more information about the skyplant is available.

Back to top

Add Plant

This page is the Add Plant page. Users can add a new plant to their house with this page. This can be done by searching the plant type or taking an image of the plant. Images can be taken or uploaded using the camera icon.

Once an image is uploded or plant name is searched, information about the plant will be displayed on the page. Clicking the place icon under the plant name allows the user to place the plant in the My House page.

Back to top

My House

This page is the My House Page. On this page, users can see all of the plants they have added to their house. A user adds all of the plants they own, allowing them to view their digital collection of plants in one location.

Each plant on this page is clickable, giving information on the plant as well as letting the user change the placement of the plant on the page. For example, clicking on the 'String of Pearls' plant will open information on this plant and its care.

Back to top

Care Calendar

This page is the Care Calendar page. The Care Calendar displays the care information for all of the plants a user adds to My House. Users can easily see their care schedule for all of their plants.

Scheduled plant reminders include: initial potting/ repotting watering, checking moisture level, and fertilizing.

Under March 2 are two icons, a water drop and a pot. At the bottom the two icons are explained: water String of Pearls and the initial potting of String of Pearls.

Under March 13 there is an icon, a drop with a percentage symbol in it. At the bottom the icon is explained: check moisture of String of Pearls.

Under March 31 there is an icon, a fertilizer bag. At the bottom the icon is explaned: fertilize String of Pearls.

Back to top

Plant Search

This is the Plant Search page. Users can use this page to learn about plants they are considering purchasing. The search page shows recent searches as well allowing the user to refine their search. To search for a plant the user can type in the plant name in the search box or image search by clicking the image icon.

Once a plant image or name is searched information about the plant is pulled up. From here the user can learn about the their plant and its care needs. Clicking on an image in the recent search also brings up the information page.
