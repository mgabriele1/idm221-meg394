Greenhouse

GreenHouse is a mobile applicaton I designed to help users care for their plants. The graphics in this prototype are hand drawn in Adobe Photoshop.

This high-fidelity prototype will help you get a feel for GreenHouse. The artwork and page links are finalized, but not everything is linked. The directions below will explain the function of each section as well as how to view the features each section.

Navigation instructions

These instructions explain how to navigate the prototype to view all of the pages and functions of the app

Home

The HOME page should be open now. If it is not click on the 'GreenHouse' text at the top of the page.

The page currently open is the Home page of GreenHouse. From this page users can access the My House, Care Calendar, Plant Search, Add Plant, and Settings pages. Each plant on the home page is also a clickable link giving information about the plant.

Navigation

Click on the highest hanging Sky Plant to the top left. Clicking on this will open a box with information on the Sky Plant. Clicking this box will bring you to the Plant Search section of the app where you can read about the skyplant. Clicking anywhere on the home page will remove this box.

Click on the 'plus plant' icon at the bottom right of the page, this will bring you to the Add Plant Page.

Add Plant

The Add Plant page should be open now. If it is not click on the 'plus plant' icon.

This page is where users can add a new plant to their house. This can be done by searching the plant type or taking an image of the plant.

Navigation

Click on the camera icon. This will take you to an example of a plant image. Click the circular button in the bottom middle to 'take the photo'. This will bring up information about the plant the app assumes your photo is. This is the correct plant, so we will place it. Click the place icon under the plant name. This will place the plant in the My House page.

Once the plant is placed, you should be on the My House page.

My House

The My House page should be open now. If it is not click the 'house' icon.

This page is where users can see all of the plants they have added to their house. This will be a collection of all the plants the user owns, allowing them to view their digital collection of plants in one location.

Navigation

Each plant on this page is clickable, giving information on the plant as well as letting the user change the placement of the plant on the page. Click on the 'String of Pearls' plant that was just placed. An information box will pop up. Click on the lower section of the box to remove it.

Click on the 'calendar' icon at the bottom of the page. This will bring you to the Care Calendar page.

Care Calendar

The Care Calendar page should be open now. If it is not click on the 'calendar' icon.

On this page, users can see their care schedule for all of their plants. Scheduled plant reminders include: initial potting/ repotting watering, checking moisture level, and fertilizing.

Navigation

The inital load page will start you on the date March 12. Click on the box for March 2. At the bottom the two icons are explained: water String of Pearls and the initial potting of String of Pearls. Click on the box for March 13. At the bottom the icon is explained: check moisture of String of Pearls. Click on the box for March 31. At the bottom the icon is explaned: fertilize String of Pearls.

Click on the 'search' icon at the bottom of the page. This will take you to the Plant Search page.

Plant Search

The Plant Search page should be open now. If it is not click on the 'search' icon.

Users can use this page to learn about plants they are considering purchasing. The search page shows recent searches as well as offering has some search. To search for a plant the user can type in the plant name in the search box or image search.

Navigation

Click on the 'camera' icon next to the search bar. This will take you to an example of a plant image. Click the circular button in the bottom middle to 'take the photo'. This will bring up information about the plant the app assumes your photo is. From here the user can learn about the String of Pearls and its care needs. Clicking anywhere on this page will bring you back to the search page. If you click on the image of the String of Pearls in the recent searches it will bring back the information page. Clicking the image of the Sky Plant in the recent searches will bring up the Sky Plant information page.

This concludes the demonstration of the GreenHouse app
